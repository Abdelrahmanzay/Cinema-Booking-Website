ABOUT US

Owned and operated by Mostafa Totos Entertainment, CineReserve is the cinema arm of Mostafa Totos, the leading shopping malls, communities, retail and leisure pioneer across the Middle East, Africa and Central Asia. CineReserve is the Middle East’s most innovative and customer-focused exhibitor, and the fastest growing cinema business in the MENA region. With 57 locations totalling 573 screens across the region, including 237 screens in the UAE, 15 screens in Lebanon, 63 screens in Oman, 30 screens in Bahrain, 44 screens in Egypt, 149 screens in Saudi Arabia and 17 screens in Kuwait, CineReserve is the Middle East’s largest and most rapidly growing exhibitor. Tailoring every cinema location to the surrounding market place and customer preferences, CineReserve offers a mix of experiences including luxury concepts, THEATRE and GOLD. Another signature CineReserve concept, MAX is the mega-screen designed for the biggest blockbusters and features Dolby Atmos surround sound technology. CineReserve was also the first to introduce IMAX with Laser to the Middle East and the only exhibitor to offer IMAX with Sapphire. The company also offers a multi-sensory movie-watching concept, 4DX. For young movie fans and families,CineReserve offers the only cinema dedicated to children, KIDS. Complementing its portfolio of world-class experiences, CineReserve offers OUTDOOR, an open-air concept.
